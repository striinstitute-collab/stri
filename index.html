Mission

STRI is dedicated to the preservation, structured reconstruction, and scholarly analysis of pre-modern covert doctrine across East Asia, with primary emphasis on Japanese shinobi manuscript traditions of the Sengoku and Edo periods.

The Shinobi Canon

The STRI Shinobi Canon consolidates primary manuscript traditions including Bansenshukai (1676), Shōninki (1681), and Ninpiden (1655) into a structured multi-volume academic series.

Methodology

STRI publications distinguish clearly between canonical manuscript text and analytical reconstruction. Where documentation is incomplete, interpretive boundaries are explicitly identified.
